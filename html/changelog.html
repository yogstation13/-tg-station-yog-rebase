10 August 2016

Cruix updated:

  • The cooldown on wizard lightning will no longer break if you ethereal jaunt or RISE! after the initial cast.
  • Camera bugs can now show camera views.
  • The chameleon masks in the chameleon kit can now have their voice changers toggled.
  • Drone lights are now deactivated for one minute by shadowling veil.
  • Minebots now have a flashlight
  • Minebots will no longer pull away from you if you are dragging them, unless they were already actively chasing a target.
  • Changing a minebot's mode will cause it to properly lose its target.
  • Sentient minebots can now toggle meson vision, their flashlight, their mode, and drop their stored ore.
  • You can once again perform heart transplants to defibrillate people whose bodies have decayed.
  • Organs once again decay when they are not inside a living body, unless the temperature is very low.
  • Freezer crates and closets once again cool their contents down enough to preserve organs or dead bodies.
  • all vending machines now have multiple categories for your browsing convenience.
  • the clerk's vending machine now contains a much wider range of products.
  • vending machine restocking units now work.
  • gift shop access can now be given out by the HoP, and the captain can now access the gift shop.
  • The EMP kit no longer claims to contain two EMP grenades even though it actually contains five.
  • Photo descriptions will now show creatures as being hurt when they are below 75% health, instead of below 75 health.
  • Fixed a bug where the nuke would sometimes not count down.
  • RCDs now give feedback when they do not have enough ammo to complete an operation.
  • Spamming an RCD to build the same wall multiple times on a tile will no longer drain all your ammo.
  • RCDs can no longer build multiple grilles on the same tile.

MacHac updated:

  • While studying the biology of slain Watchers, a Nanotrasen scientist realized that it should be impossible to freeze to death in the middle of Lavaland. Shortly thereafter, all Watchers in Lavaland switched to firing organic darts filled with a natural muscle relaxant.
  • Bolamine can now be produced in chemistry by reacting Frost Oil with Space Drugs. It slows down the user, and drains their stamina.
  • Due to an error in the syndicate agent tracker, subverted AIs were mistakenly being assigned objectives intended for humans. This has been fixed.
  • Syndicate AI subversion agents have taken an online course on subverting AIs, and can now do so slightly more consistently.

  • CTF can now be played after round ends.

  • All Nar'siean constructs can see in the dark.

  • NanoTrasen has realized that their hardsuits could be put to better use in the newfound Lavaland, and they have adapted them with fireproof plating to weather the ash storms there. Note that NanoTrasen has not spared the expense to make them lavaproof. Its price has been adjusted accordingly.
  • The wizard variant of the cursed heart, that heals the user, now has a chance to spawn in necropolis chests along with the original that does not posses healing traits.

Super3222 updated:

  • You can now fly over tables with a jetpack when gravity is down!
  • Nanotrasen has funded the production of emergencyflares for all of their employees.
  • Security has been distributed heavy-duty flares which carry more fuel than the standard addition.
  • GPS's now carry their own channel depending on the department they're coming from. So now they won't be as clustered as they use to be.
  • QM's locker now carries a mining GPS.
  • Coders now have proper OOC tags!
  • Donators now have proper OOC tags!
  • Station Charters ARE BACK!
  • The new charter is now admin dependent, meaning that the names you make up as captain will be sent to them.
  • Without any admins, the charter will not work..
  • Remember you only have one shot with your station name, so choose wisely.
  • If you feel like an admin is being unfair and not letting you choose a PERFECTLY reasonable name, then clear it up with a complaint on the forums or something of the sort (like contacting the council).
  • Space Bartenders now have access to the kitchen! Remember these are bartenders from away missions.
  • More hotkeys! ALT + CLICK ejects tapes. CTRL + CLICK plays tapes.
  • Examining sechuds will tell you the hotkeys associated with them! Remember, you need some sort of access to use them!

stealthkibbler updated:

  • Improvised bandages and their counter parts are now dropped from 100-200 crafting ticks to 45, gauze is 55 from 400. In simple terms each bandage takes 6 seconds to craft, gauze takes 8.
  • bandage heal duration has been lowered drastically from 120 seconds to 40 seconds, bandages now heal 1 damage per second to account for their 40 total heal amount.
  • Medical kit bandages heal duration is 40 seconds as-well but its worth mentioning that they heal a bit more, their heal amount being 65, meaning they are now viable meds when on the move.
