11 December 2016

AdamElTablawy updated:

  • Some asshole poured a whole beaker of deadly chemicals into the atmospherics scrubbers. Ejection of contents may be deadlier than normal.
  • You can now stabilize unstable mutation toxin with different chems to get the race you want. Check the /tg/ wiki for how. Combining different mutation toxins may have different effects.
  • Most traitor poisons have been made deadlier.
  • Neurotoxin and cyanide have been made more viable.
  • NT has updated their analyzers with better programs. Slime scanners will detect if a slime is rabid, who it's friends are, if it was fed a mutation potion, if the slime is in stasis, if it was attacked, and more. Health analyzers will alert you if your patient is having a heart attack. Analyzers will detect more gas types.
  • This isn't really related to anything but did you know that if you give a slime morphine or frost oil it slows down, and you can become better friends with a slime by feeding it plasma? Or just order it to attack people by saying 'attack (person's full name.) Seriously, I was surprised to find a lot of this stuff out.
